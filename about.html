Our Story

Rosehill Chemicals was established as the industrial formulation division of Rosehill Group to serve the growing demand for bespoke polymer solutions in construction and industrial applications. Drawing on over three decades of experience in polymer chemistry and manufacturing, we specialise in developing custom formulations that solve complex technical challenges.

Based in our state-of-the-art facility in West Yorkshire, we combine traditional chemical expertise with modern analytical capabilities to deliver innovative solutions that meet the exacting demands of today's industries.

Our Team

Our skilled team of chemists, engineers, and technical specialists bring decades of combined experience in polymer science and industrial applications.

Senior Chemists

PhD-qualified polymer chemists with extensive experience in polyurethane, epoxy, and hybrid polymer systems. Expert knowledge in reaction mechanisms and formulation chemistry.

Process Engineers

Skilled in scale-up and manufacturing processes, ensuring seamless transition from laboratory development to commercial production whilst maintaining quality.

Analytical Specialists

Advanced analytical capabilities including spectroscopy, thermal analysis, and mechanical testing to validate performance and ensure consistency.

Application Experts

Deep understanding of real-world applications and performance requirements across construction, industrial, and specialist markets.

Our West Yorkshire Facility

Our purpose-built facility houses state-of-the-art equipment for formulation development, testing, and production, ensuring we can support projects from concept through to commercial scale.

R&D Laboratory

Fully equipped chemistry laboratory with fume extraction, analytical instruments, and controlled environment testing capabilities.

Pilot Plant

Flexible production equipment for batch sizes from 1kg to 5 tonnes, enabling seamless scale-up from laboratory to commercial production.

Quality Control

Comprehensive testing facilities including mechanical testing, thermal analysis, and environmental conditioning chambers.

Analytical Services

Advanced analytical capabilities including FTIR spectroscopy, GPC, and DSC for complete product characterisation.
