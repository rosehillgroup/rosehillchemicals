Where Complex Chemistry Meets Practical Solutions

Rosehill Chemicals is the industrial formulation division of Rosehill Group, specialising in the design and manufacture of custom polymer systems. From pre-polymers and binder technologies to structural adhesives and hybrid sealants, we partner with manufacturers, contractors, and OEMs to develop high-performance materials for construction and industrial applications.

Whether you're facing a bonding challenge, require unique mechanical properties, or need a reactive system to suit a specific environment, we work with you to create a solution that performs — without compromise.

Tailored Formulation Capabilities

Polyurethane Systems

  • MDI-based and low-free monomer systems
  • One- and two-component formulations
  • Custom cure profiles and pot life
  • Flexible to rigid elastomers
  • Spray and cast applications

Adhesives & Sealants

  • Structural bonding systems
  • Construction joint sealants
  • Hybrid polymer technologies
  • Silane-modified systems (STP)
  • Low-VOC and solvent-free options

Custom Pre-polymers

  • Bespoke reactive intermediates
  • Controlled molecular weights
  • Specialty end-capping
  • Custom functionality
  • Scalable from lab to production

Reactive Repair Systems

  • Structural repair compounds
  • Crack injection systems
  • Concrete bonding agents
  • Protective coatings
  • Environmental resistance

Where Our Materials Perform

Our formulations are used in a wide range of demanding applications — from structural bonding and flooring systems to protective coatings and impact-resistant components. Whether your project requires chemical resistance, UV stability, flexibility, or mechanical strength, we create systems that meet exacting performance standards in real-world conditions.

Construction & Infrastructure

Structural adhesives, joint sealants, repair systems, and protective coatings for building and civil engineering projects.

Industrial Manufacturing

Assembly adhesives, component bonding systems, and specialised elastomers for manufacturing processes.

Infrastructure Maintenance

Repair compounds, protective coatings, and remedial systems for existing infrastructure and equipment.

Specialist Applications

Custom formulations for unique challenges requiring specific performance characteristics.
