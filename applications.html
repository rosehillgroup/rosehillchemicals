Applications & Industries

Bespoke formulations solving complex challenges across construction, manufacturing, and infrastructure sectors

From structural bonding in demanding environments to specialised repair systems for aging infrastructure, our custom polymer formulations deliver proven performance where it matters most. We work across diverse industries, developing solutions that meet exacting standards whilst addressing the unique challenges of each application.

Industries We Serve

Construction & Infrastructure

Structural bonding, sealing, and repair solutions for building and civil engineering

  • Structural Glazing: High-strength sealants for curtain walls and façade systems
  • Concrete Repair: Crack injection systems and structural strengthening compounds
  • Waterproofing: Membrane adhesives and liquid-applied systems
  • Joint Sealing: Movement joint sealants for bridges and buildings
  • Flooring Systems: Polyurethane screeds and seamless floor coatings
  • Protective Coatings: Anti-carbonation and chemical-resistant systems
  • Anchor Systems: Structural adhesives for post-installed fixings

Industrial Manufacturing

Assembly adhesives and component bonding systems for manufacturing processes

  • Component Assembly: Structural adhesives replacing mechanical fasteners
  • Composite Bonding: Adhesives for fibre-reinforced plastic assemblies
  • Potting Compounds: Encapsulation systems for electronic components
  • Gasket Materials: Custom elastomers for sealing applications
  • Tooling Systems: Mould-making compounds and casting resins
  • Protective Coatings: Chemical-resistant linings for tanks and vessels
  • Vibration Damping: Elastomeric systems for noise and vibration control

Infrastructure Maintenance

Repair and rehabilitation systems for existing structures and equipment

  • Bridge Repair: Structural strengthening with carbon fibre systems
  • Concrete Restoration: Bonding agents and repair mortars
  • Pipe Rehabilitation: Lining systems for water and sewer pipes
  • Corrosion Protection: Primer and coating systems for steel structures
  • Emergency Repairs: Rapid-setting systems for urgent situations
  • Weatherproofing: Restoration sealants for aging buildings
  • Deck Waterproofing: Bridge and car park protection systems

Specialist Applications

Custom formulations for unique challenges requiring specific performance characteristics

  • Marine Environments: Saltwater-resistant adhesives and coatings
  • Fire-Resistant Systems: Intumescent sealants and protective coatings
  • Food-Safe Applications: FDA-compliant sealants and coatings
  • Extreme Temperatures: Formulations for -40°C to +150°C service
  • Chemical Resistance: Acid and solvent-resistant systems
  • Electrical Insulation: Dielectric potting and encapsulation compounds
  • Underwater Curing: Moisture-tolerant adhesives and sealants
